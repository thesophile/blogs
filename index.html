Art, Literature and Meaning

What is poetry? When I was little, I thought it was just a little dance with pretty poetic words - Similies, Metaphors, beutiful descriptions… In a sense thats true, It is poetic to compare grief with a gloomy sky. But comparing object A to Object B is not the part which makes it a poem. Lets look at some examples of poetic phrases/sentence: A fallen flower compared to the impermanence of beauty.1 Season-emotions: example - summer to Happy, winter to grief etc. Daily life comparisons - “My head is exploding”, “I am drowning in work” “A weight lifted off my shoulders.” “The color drained from my world.” We use these so often in out lives so that we have to keep saying “literally” when the words really mean what they are saying. Because for us humans, words are not just a way to communicate physical states or feelings but also emotional ones. They help to communicate what we are feeling. That is why poetry often comes around in our daily lives, even without us noticing. ...
